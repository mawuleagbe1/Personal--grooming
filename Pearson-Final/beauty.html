Oval Face Makeup Tips

For a fresh, youthful makeup look, start by focusing blush on the apples of your cheeksâ€”smile lightly to locate them, then blend upward toward your temples for a natural flush. Next, highlight the center of your forehead and chin with a luminous product to brighten your complexion and create dimension, drawing attention to the middle of your face

Round Face Makeup Tips

For Round faces, contouring is key. Apply a matte bronzer along the sides of your forehead and under your cheekbones to create the illusion of length. Use a highlighter on the center of your forehead, nose, and chin to bring attention to the middle of your face. For blush, apply it slightly above the apples of your cheeks and blend diagonally towards your temples for a lifted effect.
