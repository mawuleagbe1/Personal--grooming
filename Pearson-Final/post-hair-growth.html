The Truth About Hair Growth: Myths vs. Science-Backed Facts

Michael Chen Certified Trichologist

Hair growth comparrson

The hair growth industry is flooded with misinformation. As a trichologist with 15 years experience, I'm debunking 10 persistent myths and revealing what actually works based on clinical studies (evidencebased) and hair biology (folliclescience).

Myth Busting: The Big 10

❌ Cutting makes hair grow faster

✅ The Truth:

Hair grows from roots, not ends. Regular trims prevent split ends (breakage) but don't affect growth rate (follicleactivity).

❌ Brushing 100 strokes daily stimulates growth

✅ The Truth:

Over-brushing causes mechanical damage (friction). Gentle detangling is sufficient (scalpbloodflow).

Clinically Proven Growth Methods

①

Topical Minoxidil

How it works: Vasodilator prolongs anagen phase (prostaglandins)

Evidence: 85% show reduced shedding in 4-6 months (Journal of Dermatology)

Dermatologist Tip: Start with 2% if sensitive (scalpirritation), apply to dry scalp (absorption)

②

Microneedling

How it works: 0.5-1.5mm needles stimulate growth factors (WNTproteins)

Protocol: Weekly sessions with dermaroller (sterilize), followed by peptides

When to Consider Supplements

  • Diagnosed deficiency (bloodtest)
  • Vegetarian/vegan diets (b12)
  • Postpartum shedding (ferritin)

⚠️ Supplement Warning

Excess selenium (toxicity) and vitamin A (hypervitaminosis) can cause hair loss. Always test first.

Month 3-4
