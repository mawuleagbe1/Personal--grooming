The 5 Essential Products

1

Tinted Moisturizer/BB Cream

For Dry Skin:

Ilia Super Serum Skin Tint (skincarefirst) with hyaluronic acid and squalane

For Oily Skin:

Fenty Beauty Eaze Drop Blurring Skin Tint (oilcontrol) with blurring pigments

Cream Blush & Lip Color

Cheeks: Smile and dab on apples (freshflushed)

  • Peach: Warm undertones (universal)

The 5-Minute Application Guide

0:00-1:00

Base Even Out

Press tinted moisturizer into skin starting from center (lessismore). No need for full coverage‚Äîlet freckles show through (skinfirst).

1:00-2:30

Color Placement

Apply cream blush to cheeks, lips, and eyelids (multitasker). Build intensity gradually (sheertopigmented).
